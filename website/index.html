About the App

Beach Grain Sense is a mobile app that lets you capture sand images, automatically detects a currency note for scale, and uses AI to analyze and classify sand grain size. Perfect for students, researchers, and beach lovers!

  • Take a photo of sand with a currency note for scale
  • Get instant grain size analysis and classification
  • Works offline after first install
  • Open source and privacy-friendly
